Nanny Co is focused on safety

Our dedicated team is working hard behind the scenes every day. For more information about our commitment to safety, Trust and Safety Center .

Buy background checks

Buying background checks is a necessary investment for your nanny business, providing a comprehensive review of a caregiver's criminal history and other relevant information. By purchasing background checks, you can make informed hiring decisions and prioritize the safety and well-being of the children in your care.

Identity verification

Identity verification is a vital process that confirms the authenticity of a person's identity, ensuring that caregivers are who they claim to be. Through identity verification, your nanny business can establish trust with families and provide an added layer of security in the hiring process.

Fraud prevention

Fraud prevention measures are essential in protecting your nanny business from deceitful activities, such as identity theft or false credentials. By implementing fraud prevention strategies, you can safeguard your business and families from potential scams and ensure a secure hiring process.

Safety screenings

Safety screenings are a crucial step in ensuring the well-being of children in your care, involving thorough checks on potential caregivers to identify any red flags or concerns. By conducting safety screenings, you can have peace of mind knowing that your nanny business is providing a safe and trustworthy environment for families.
