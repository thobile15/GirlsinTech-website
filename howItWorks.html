  • How it works?

1. How do I book a nanny?

To book a nanny, simply visit our website and create an account.
Once logged in, you can browse available nannies, view their profiles,
and schedule appointments based on your requirements.

2. What qualifications do your nannies have?

All our nannies undergo thorough background checks and have relevant certifications
in childcare, such as CPR and first aid training.
We also ensure that they have prior experience working with children.

3. Can I request a specific nanny?

Yes, you can request a specific nanny based on their availability.
Simply contact us and let us know your preferences,
and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

4. What are your rates?

Our rates vary depending on the type of service you require and the
duration of the booking. Please refer to our pricing page for more information,
or contact us directly for a personalized quote.

5. How do I pay for nanny services?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and electronic bank
transfers. Payment details will be provided upon booking confirmation.
