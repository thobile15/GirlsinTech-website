1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer or mobile device)
when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well
as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How do we use cookies?

At NANNY CO., we use cookies to improve your browsing experience and provide personalized services.
We may use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies
(which remain on your device for a set period of time).

Specifically, we use cookies for:

  • Authenticating users and maintaining user sessions.
  • Remembering your preferences and settings.
  • Analyzing site traffic and usage patterns.
  • Customizing content and advertising.

3. How to control cookies?

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings.
Most web browsers allow you to block or
delete cookies, as well as to set preferences for how cookies are handled. However, please note
that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website and limit certain
features and functionalities.

4. Changes to this policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other
operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically
for any updates or changes.
